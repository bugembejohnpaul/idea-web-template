Explore Youth livelihood

Our youth livelihood initiatives focus on empowering young people by providing vocational training, financial literacy education, and entrepreneurship opportunities. We aim to equip them with practical skills and knowledge needed to succeed in various trades and businesses, promote sound financial management practices, and support them in starting and growing their own ventures. By investing in their development, we strive to enhance their economic prospects and help them build a more secure and prosperous future.
